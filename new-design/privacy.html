Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2026

SwiftLift does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your workouts stay on your device. Full stop.

What We Collect

Nothing. SwiftLift does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other data from your device.

All workout data you create within the app (sessions, exercises, sets, weights, reps) is stored exclusively in your iPhone's local storage and is never transmitted to any server.

Accounts

SwiftLift does not require or support user accounts of any kind. We have no database of users. We do not know you exist unless you email us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through their StoreKit framework. SwiftLift never sees your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Third-Party Services

SwiftLift does not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or tracking libraries. None.

Data Security

Because we never collect your data, there is nothing for us to secure on our end. Your data is protected by your device's own security, including Face ID, Touch ID, and iPhone encryption.

Children

SwiftLift does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children. The app does not collect any data from any user regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the date above and note the changes in the App Store release notes. We don't anticipate changes — our privacy philosophy is fundamental to the product.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@swiftlift.app
