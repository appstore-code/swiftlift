Support

We keep things simple — here are answers to the most common questions. Can't find yours? Email us directly.

How do I restore my purchase after reinstalling?
Open SwiftLift → go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchase." Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID, so it restores instantly. No account needed.
Where is my workout data stored?
Everything is stored locally on your iPhone. No cloud, no servers, no accounts. Your data is 100% private and lives only on your device.
I switched to a new iPhone. Can I transfer my data?
If you use iPhone's built-in backup (iCloud Backup or a Mac/PC backup) and restore from that backup to your new device, SwiftLift and your data will transfer automatically.
Is there a subscription?
No. Never. SwiftLift is free to download with a single optional one-time purchase. We will never introduce a subscription model.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email us directly at the address below. Include your iOS version and what happened. We take bugs seriously and respond fast.
